About us

Your hub for digital games

Game Store is a demo digital marketplace where independent sellers can list their games and players can safely browse, purchase, and build a personal library. The project showcases a modern cloud-ready architecture built with Python, Flask, and relational storage.

What we focus on

This platform is designed to feel like a real game launcher and store:

  • Buyer accounts to browse, add to cart, and purchase games.
  • Seller accounts with dashboards for listing and managing titles.
  • Secure order flow and a clean, sci-fi themed user interface.
